Rewards Analyst CZ, SK & HU

Odpovědět

This role provides comprehensive support to key reward initiatives and programs in the cluster of Czech Republic, Slovakia & Hungary including annual compensation process, salary reviews, bonus and incentive schemes, benefits, mobility and recognition programs. Analyzes jobs and reviews job descriptions and specifications, applies job evaluation techniques to establish equitable compensation rates within the organization as compared with rates in industry.

What you’ll do?

• Provide support to key reward initiatives and program in cluster level (annual compensation process, salary reviews, bonus schemes, recognition programs);
• analyze jobs and evaluate job descriptions;
• conduct and analyze salary surveys to price jobs according to market trends to remain competitive;
• develop of compensation policies, procedures in order to be responsive to company's goals;
• work closely with HRBPs, HR Operations, Finance team to support people cost reporting & forecasting process;
• administer regional and local benefit programs, maintain vendor relations;
• prepare and maintain standard and ad hoc reports, gain all key reward information;
• actively contribute to wellbeing and recognition initiatives.




What should be your background and skills?

• have secondary education degree (university degree in economics is a notable advantage);
• gained 1-2 years experience in HR or finance in similar position;
• advanced user of Excel (experience in Pivot tables is an advantage);
• excellent command in Czech/Slovak and English (written and verbal form as well);
• strong analytical thinking with good deep dives into numbers;
• you are accurate and focus of high quality in results;
• you are experienced how to operate with highly confidential and sensitive information with diplomacy;
• you are able to communicate technical information to non-technical users clearly and effectively;
• you are familiar with job evaluation and market pricing technics is preferred;

What is in it for you?

• Great beer, brands and teams around the globe to work with every day;
• Pivovary Staropramen, s. r. o. is a part of Molson Coors Beverage Company;
• Staropramen is well recognized international brand, No. 1 Prague beer;
• A dynamic and fast moving international company that loves to win;
• Super-interesting work that is complex, challenging and fun all at the same time;
• Opportunity for career growth and development along with competitive benefits.
