We are an automotive supplier, a partner to all automakers worldwide. As a technological company, we propose innovative products and systems that contribute to reduction of CO2 emissions and to development of automated driving.
Since 2002 we have been developing thermal systems in our R&D Center in Prague. Currently we are working on high-voltage components for electric vehicles' thermal management. Our team of mechanical, test and system engineers works closely with our development center in La Verrière, France. We manage our own projects and are pioneers in the electric mobility market due to our innovative products.

Validation Engineer - Thermal Systems

Locality: Strašnice

Employment type: Full-time work

Apply

We are looking for a VALIDATION ENGINEER who will be a part of our strong R&D center focused on development of cutting edge technological products which are used in electrical cars.

YOUR CHALLENGES?

  • Be responsible for complete management of specific tests
  • Participation in defintion test cases to cover testing of product requirements
  • Be involved in developing test plans considering laboratory resources
  • Archive the results in a database and write internal validation reports of tests
  • Participate in the monitoring tests of series products
  • Participate in writing of follow-up of functional procedures, test methods and laboratory indications
  • Report validation status to the project team and management on a regular basis
  • The Decree No. 50/78 Sb $6 or higher

LET'S TALK ABOUT YOU...

  • University or secondary technical education
  • Experience with Validation Field
  • Experience with Environmental testing
  • Team work / team player
  • Good communication level in English is advantage
  • Customer oriented
  • Knowledge of industrial drawing,mechanical tests and quality tools is advantageous
  • Physical measures, acquisition, testing

WE OFFER:

  • Work in a stable international company in a multinational team on challenging projects with huge impact on the whole automotive industry
  • Automotive environment with cutting-edge technologies
  • Cooperation with Valeo development sites worldwide
  • Possibility to see the whole development process in one place from an idea (customer requirement) to a product testing and manufacture
  • Personal development through special trainings and language courses
  • Competitive package of benefits including 6 weeks of vacation plus other paid or non-paid days off, flexible working hours and home office possibility, variable bonuses - monthly and semi-annual, a bonus for an innovative idea, a bonus for a life or working anniversary, a bonus for recommendation of a new colleague up to 40 000 CZK, contribution to the pension insurance after the probation period (5% from gross salary, max. 4166 CZK), meal and free time vouchers, mobility support, company canteen, easy access to work by Prague public transportation and company car park, a fitness room and relax zones, sports ground (football, basketball…) and barbecue gazebo, company’s events, free tickets for IQ Landia and others.
